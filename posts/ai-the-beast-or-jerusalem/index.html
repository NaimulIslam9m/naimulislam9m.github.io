AI: The Beast or Jerusalem?

January 9, 2023 3-minute read
AI • Podcast • Philosophy

Here I’ve shared my notes what I’ve taken from AI: The Beast or Jerusalem? video in my own words.

  • It is estimated that human brain can do $10^{18}$ Operations/Second. It could be wrong but it is estimated based on different parameters.
  • Modern CPUs on our phone can do ~$10^{10}$ Operations/Second (10 Billion). And GPUs can do ~$10^{11}$ Operations/Second (100 Billion).
  • Modern AIs like Google or OpenAI are doing $10^{16}$ Operations/Second.
  • How AI is summarizing the text?
    • How we get the gits of a story? We read it and try to predict it from our previous knowledge. Some times our prediction is correct and sometimes not. That’s the twist of a good story. AI do the same thing. It predicts what’s next line going to be. If the prediction is correct then its awesome. If its not correct AI learns from it.
    • So, if you feed AI with the text of multiple genre then it can create absolutely new genre of text or insight what no one have written before.
  • Compare learning of AI with a new born baby. It doesn’t know how world works. It sees, hears and learns from what others are doing. And our learning is how neurons are interacting with each other. Our whole consciousness depends on how one neuron is interacting with other. Same with AI. Its just in its baby form. Making some mistakes and learning from it. Surely soon It will come in a point where It will have consciousness like human does.
  • Jim said, “Within 5/10 years most peoples best friend will be AI”. They will know you very well and will support you in your bad time.
  • Super computer used to cost millions of dollars now our phone is that super computer. Time between millions of dollars and 10$ is 30 years.
  • We’re 10 years away from seeing computer’s replicating itself.
  • Why humans creating something that will go beyond them?
    • With respect to power and money someone is already superior than us. So what’s wrong with more intelligent artificial being.
  • Can humans and AI coexist?
    • Why not? Different animals are coexisting with humans peacefully. This animal kingdom depends on mutual benefit. So we might go in a situation where AI will do their thing and Humans will do their thing.
  • The literature on marital stability indicates that there is a sweet spot with respect to marital stability in terms of the ratio of negative and positive communication. On average if your positive and negative communication ratio is 5:1 with your spouse, that’s low threshold of stability. If its 4:1, you’re headed to divorce. On the other end, if it exceeds 11 positive and 1 negative you’re also moving towards divorce.
